About QSTRA

Our Belief

Finance is broken. Power is concentrated, access is gated, and alpha is hidden behind closed doors. We believe that the future of capital is on-chain, community-powered, and fully transparent.

The Role of $QSTRA

$QSTRA is the fuel behind the QSTRA ecosystem. It powers governance, unlocks capital access, and rewards participation. Strategy creators and investors alike are incentivized through staking, airdrops, and performance-based emissions.

Capital Allocation

Capital is pooled from DAO backers and allocated based on performance metrics and community vote. Each vault’s size is governed by the DAO, allowing for transparent and dynamic rebalancing of capital between strategies.

How We Fund Strategies

Staked $QSTRA determines access to strategy capital. The more you stake, the more influence and rewards you receive. Vaults that perform earn more allocation — those that don’t are rotated out. This dynamic allocation ensures efficiency and fairness.

Deflation by Design

A portion of profits generated by high-performing vaults is used to buy and burn $QSTRA, reducing token supply over time. This creates upward pressure on the value of $QSTRA as the protocol scales.

Rewards for Participation

Community members who stake $QSTRA are rewarded through airdrops, boosted yield access, and participation in DAO decision-making. Contributors, voters, and strategists alike all share in the protocol’s success.
