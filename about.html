About QSTRA

QSTRA is a decentralized, DAO-governed quant ecosystem where alpha is encrypted, performance is rewarded, and access is earned.

Why We Exist

We believe the future of trading is encrypted, permissionless, and driven by merit — not marketing. QSTRA funds the smartest, most promising strategies without ever seeing the code.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Encrypted strategy submissions
  • On-chain performance verification
  • DAO-voted allocations
  • Real yield shared with contributors

For Quants

Submit your strategy, keep your code private, and let performance speak for itself. We handle the rest — deployment, monitoring, rewards.

For Backers

Support alpha discovery, earn DAO governance power, and gain access to the next wave of financial infrastructure.

How QSTRA Works

1. Strategy Submission

Developers securely upload encrypted trading strategies — QSTRA never sees the raw code.

2. Performance Testing & DAO Voting

Each strategy is sandbox tested. Verified performance data is shared transparently with the DAO for token-weighted voting.

3. On-Chain Deployment

Top-voted strategies receive capital. They’re deployed live with results tracked transparently on-chain.

4. Rewards & Reputation

Quants earn $QSTRA and performance yield. Backers benefit from aligned upside. Reputation grows on a public leaderboard.
