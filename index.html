About QSTRA

QSTRA is the world's first decentralized quant platform. We connect trading signal creators with capital without ever revealing their code. Governed by a DAO, QSTRA funds top-performing, encrypted strategies across crypto, equities, and FX.

How It Works

1. Submit your encrypted strategy
2. DAO ranks & votes
3. Top strategies receive deployment capital
4. Quants earn yield + $QSTRA rewards
5. Leaderboard + token-based rep grows over time
